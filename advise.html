PERSONAL BELONGINGS TO SCHOOL

To ensure good hygiene and for students to feel as at home, parents should providethe following items for their child on the first day of shool:

  1. CHILD UNDER 24 MONTHS OLD
    • A picture of her/his family in order to make a familiar feeling for her/him
    • Her/his favourite toy or stuff animal if she/he needs
    • One plastic cup for drinking water
    • One plastic cup for bushing teeth
    • One hat for going to outdoor
    • Two sets of uniforms, one set of pyzama
    • Five soft small towers (for daily use)
    • Nappies
    • Milk Feed bottles
  2. CHILD OVER 24 MONTHS OLD
    • A picture of her/his family in order to make a familiar feeling for her/him
    • Her/his favourite toy or stuff animal if she/he needs
    • One plastic cup for drinking water
    • One plastic cup for bushing teeth
    • One hat for going to outdoor
    • Two sets of uniforms, one set of pyzama
    • One small towers (for daily use)

In order to avoid missing of children's belongings, parents need to write thechild's name on all items.
